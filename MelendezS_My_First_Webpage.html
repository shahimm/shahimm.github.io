Education you need

There is no specific college degree required to play professional basketball. However, to play college basketball, you must meet academic requirements. To play in the NBA, you must meet certain age requirements and other eligibility criteria.

Health Benefits

Playing basketball has many health benefits, including improved cardiovascular health, increased bone density, and improved balance. It can also help you build muscle and burn calories.

How did basketball oringinate

Basketball was invented in 1891 by James Naismith, a physical education teacher at the International YMCA Training School in Springfield, Massachusetts. Naismith created the game to keep students active during the winter when outdoor sports were not possible.
